Professional Background

What is a Social Worker in Private Practice?

Social Workers in Private Practice are trained Profesionals who offer their services directly to individuals, families, groups and organisations on a fee for service basis.

A Social Worker Profession:

Social work is a wide ranging profession. Social workers seek to bring about empowerment and change in the lives of the individuals and families and communities they serve. They offer counselling, psychotherapy and mediation services to individuals, families and groups to effect psycho-social change. Some social workers are engaged in bringing about broad based changes in organisation and communities by means of community work methods and development strategies. social work services are offered in multiple settings from welfare agencies, to school, to hospitals and prisons, to corporate settings.
